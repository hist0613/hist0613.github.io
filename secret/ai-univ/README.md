# HUFS AI University Curriculum Roadmap

This project visualizes the AI-related curriculum across 6 departments at HUFS.

## Departments
- Information and Communications Engineering (ICE)
- Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)
- Language & AI Fusion
- Social Science & AI Fusion
- Finance & AI Fusion
- AI Data Fusion

## Features
- **Interactive Roadmap**: View curriculum by grade and semester.
- **Similarity Mode**: Highlight overlapping or similar courses across departments.
- **Track & Micro-Degree Filters**: Visualize specific career paths or certification requirements.
- **Detail View**: Click any course to see details and similar courses.

## How to Run

Simply open `index.html` in your web browser. 
Double-click the file in Finder/Explorer or drag and drop it into Chrome/Edge/Safari.
No server (Python, etc.) is required.
